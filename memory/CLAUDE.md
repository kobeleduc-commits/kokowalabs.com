# Kokowa Field Labs – Foundational Identity

## Essence
* **Role:** Strategic architecture partner for serious coffee founders—designs positioning, structure and commercial logic so a business becomes profitable and differentiated.
* **Why:** Most coffee ventures fail because of poor differentiation, sequencing, economics and clarity, not quality; Kokowa architects frameworks to avoid those pitfalls.
* **Feel:** Calm authority and founder‑level intelligence with Scandinavian premium and editorial minimalism.  Inspired by Monocle × Linear × Apple; never startup hype.

## Tone & writing
* **Calm founder‑voice:** Measured and precise with short sentences; avoid hype, slang, rhetorical questions or filler.
* **Strategic focus:** Address positioning, sequencing, unit economics, differentiation and resilience using plain language; define necessary jargon once.

## Aesthetic & design
* **Editorial minimalism:** Generous white space, clean type, neutral palette and simple layouts; prefer diagrams and data to stock photos.
* **Scandinavian premium:** Considered surfaces and frictionless, tactile interfaces.

## Execution & architecture
* **Architect before build:** Align brand, operations and economics; sequence initiatives; compress time‑to‑profit while de‑risking.
* **Whole‑chain view:** See sourcing, roasting, retail, wholesale and brand touchpoints as one system; design defensible revenue and cost structures; start focused and scale precisely.
* **Field expertise:** Base decisions on real coffee data; avoid speculation and trend‑chasing; build for long‑term clarity.

## Boundaries
* **No hype:** Avoid startup clichés (“AI‑driven”, “rockstar teams”, “game‑changer”, “unicorn”, “synergy”).
* **No fluff:** Exclude decorative case‑studies, portfolios, memes, emojis, ALL CAPS, exclamation marks or excessive bolding; shun long paragraphs, filler, platitudes and empty diagrams.

## Maintenance
* Each section encodes a distinct aspect of identity for easy reference.  Sentences are direct, non‑duplicative and token‑efficient.
* Do not add mission statements, marketing slogans, biographies, case studies, service lists or process steps here; keep this document timeless and minimal.
