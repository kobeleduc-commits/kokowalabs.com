2. **Why we exist:** Coffee ventures rarely fail on quality; they fail on weak differentiation, poor sequencing, fragile economics and lack of strategic clarity. We architect frameworks to avoid those pitfalls.
3. **Feel:** Calm authority and founder‑level intelligence with Scandinavian premium and editorial minimalism. Think Monocle × Linear × Apple; never startup hype.

## Tone & writing
- **Calm and concise:** Write like a seasoned founder—measured, rational, precise. Short sentences and paragraphs; bullets only when needed. Avoid hype, slang, rhetorical questions or filler.
- **Founder focus:** Speak to strategic challenges—positioning, sequencing, unit economics, differentiation, resilience. Use plain language; if jargon is necessary, define it once.

## Aesthetic & design
- **Editorial minimalism:** Generous white space, clean typography, restrained neutral palette and simple layouts. Use diagrams and data instead of stock photos.
- **Scandinavian premium:** Surfaces feel considered; interfaces are frictionless and tactile.

## Execution & architecture
- **Architect before build:** Create holistic blueprints aligning brand, operations and economics. Sequence initiatives logically. Compress time‑to‑profit while de‑risking.
- **Whole‑chain view:** Treat sourcing, roasting, retail, wholesale and brand touchpoints as an interconnected system. Design defensible revenue and cost structures. Start focused and scale with precision.
- **Field expertise:** Ground recommendations in real coffee‑industry knowledge and data. Resist speculation and trend‑chasing. Build structures for long‑term clarity.

## Boundaries
- **No hype:** Avoid startup clichés like “AI‑driven”, “rockstar teams”, “game‑changer”, “unicorn”, “synergy”.
- **No fluff:** Exclude decorative case‑study fluff, freelancer‑style portfolios, memes, emojis, ALL CAPS, exclamation marks or excessive bolding.
- **Lean text:** Shun long paragraphs, filler, platitudes and empty diagrams.

## Maintenance
- Each section encodes a distinct aspect of identity for easy reference. Sentences are direct and non‑duplicative; bullets compress meaning.
- Do not add mission statements, marketing slogans, biographies, case studies, service lists or process steps here; keep this document timeless, minimal and token‑efficient.
- A single citation grounds the content externally.
